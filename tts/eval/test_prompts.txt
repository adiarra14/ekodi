# Bambara (Bamanankan) test prompts for TTS evaluation
# One prompt per line. Lines starting with # are comments.

# ── Greetings ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
I ni ce!
I ni tile!
Aw ni ce!
I ka kɛnɛ wa?
Tɔɔrɔ si tɛ.
Hɛɛrɛ dɔrɔn.

# ── Common phrases ────────────────────────────────────────────
N bɛ bamanankan mɛn.
A bɛ taa sugu la.
Den misɛnw bɛ taa kalanso la.
Bamanankan ye kan ɲuman ye.
Mali ye jamana ɲuman ye.

# ── Slightly longer sentences ─────────────────────────────────
Ne bɛ fɛ ka bamanankan kalan, bari a ka di n ye kosɛbɛ.
Sugu bɛ da kɛnɛma, mɔgɔw bɛ feere ni san ye.
Tile bɛ bɔ kɔrɔn fɛ, a bɛ bin tlɛ fɛ.

# ── Questions ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
I tɔgɔ ye mun ye?
E bɛ bɔ min?
O ye mun ye?
